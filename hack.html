BitDust project

We are aiming to develop an open-source and free computer software which gives you full control over your personal data and private communications and is 100% secure and bulletproof.

How BitDust Works

Research

The BitDust project explores the possibilities for introducing new technologies in society to protect private information on the network.

Experiment

BitDust enthusiasts are focused to create a new generation distributed network for secure data storage and private communications in the net.

Learn

We have been sharing our developments for a long time, and now we decided to organize a first on-line hackcaton for everyone who is interested in this area.

The Challenge

Design, develop and submit a solution within 48 hours that makes the greatest contribution to the development of the BitDust project and the overall mission of BitDust
