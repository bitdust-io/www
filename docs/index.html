BitDust project

We are developing an open-source and free computer software which gives you full control over your personal data and private communications and is 100% secure and bulletproof.

  • Research

    BitDust project explores the possibilities for introducing new technologies in society to protect private information on the network.

  • Experiment

    BitDust enthusiasts are focused to create a new generation distributed network for secure data storage and private communications in the net.

  • Learn

    We have been sharing our developments for a long time, and now we decided to organize a first on-line hackcaton for everyone who is interested in this area.

The Challenge
