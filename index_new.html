How BitDust Works

The product

BitDust provides a free-to-use open source application for Windows, Mac and Linux where by you can safely and securely upload and manage your personal data. In addition it offers you the ability to chat and share files with your friends. Give it a try!

The problem

Nowadays we create a lot of personal data like pictures, text files and movies. However we all store them at centralized entities. We put trust in centralized authorities to keep our most intimate data safe and secure. Nevertheless once uploaded we don’t have any real control over it.

The solution

We off course say in this section that BitDust is the answer to the problem:). In addition we wanted to provide the same key feature images like distributed storage. You need to find the icons for the following aspects:

  • Distributed storage

    Independent, safe and private. Fully under your control.

  • Encrypted

    All data is fully encrypted using RSA & DSE3 encryption. Your private key which only you have provides you with the power to unlock your data.

  • Anonymous

    Set-up an account and use BitDust completely anonymous.

  • Chat

    Start fully encrypted chats with your friends.

  • Share

    You can easily share files with your friends.

  • Redundant

    BitDust copies your data twice to increase redundancy.

The ecosystem

In this section we will explain the ecosystem. The ecosystem consists mainly out of two elements.

  • Users

    These are people that will install the consumer application of BitDust.

  • Supporters

    These are people that support the BitDust network in the role of Supplier, Rebuilder or miner. If you can make a split between the two that would be great so on the left you will have users including an icon, text and a download button. On the left you will have supporters with an icon of supplier+text, Miner+text and rebuilder+text. As the supporting roles need a bit more explanation for people to know if they qualify we need to redirect them. So below the quick overview of the supporting roles you will have a button with “more information”.
